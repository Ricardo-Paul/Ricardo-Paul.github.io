Nostalgia

Ricardo Paul
On
June 7, 2024

The internet today can be a noisy place, filled with drama, sensationalism and shallowness.
I’ve been feeling nostalgic lately about what the web looked like in the early 2000s.

I am not old enough to have witnessed these good old days when people would write and share their thoughts - pretty much just for the sake of it - without obsessing over attracting visitors, clicks and likes.

The internet has evolved and become a lot more sophisticated today, and I’m grateful for all of it. But I can’t help but miss the authenticity and depth that defined the web in the early days.
