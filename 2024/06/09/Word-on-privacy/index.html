Word on privacy; a reason for so few notes here

Ricardo Paul
On
June 9, 2024

This blog was born in 2021 out of a need to talk, to document a journey and to reflect on my own life. I initially thought of it as some kind of live journal where I’d be able to write about everything without regard to privacy. But it became pretty clear very fast that not everything belongs in a public journal so I turned to more private means.

A lot of notes that were meant to be published here somehow got way too personal and wound up instead in a private diary. And so from 2021 to this day, hundreds of journal entries have been recorded on electronic devices and on paper - those that eventually make their way to the blog have been cherry-picked.
