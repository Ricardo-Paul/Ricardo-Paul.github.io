Be a monster, maybe

Ricardo Paul
On
July 11, 2024

Listening to part of a Jordan Peterson’s lecture on why you should be a monster.

What he says sounds dangerously true and I’m quoting - “If you are not able of cruelty, you’re absolutely a victim of everyone who is”.

He also says “…being able to be cruel and then not being cruel is better than not being able to be cruel. Because in the first case, you’re nothing but weak and naive and in the second case, you’re dangerous but you have it under control.”

Ahhhhghr, unpopular belief but how true !
