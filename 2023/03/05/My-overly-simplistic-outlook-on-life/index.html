My overly simplistic outlook on life

Ricardo Paul
On
March 5, 2023

In less than 80 years, you probably won’t exist. You will have already died. So will I. That’s just to emphasize how short life is.

People who are seemingly unaware of this truth tend to attach too much importance to the wrong things in my view, like money or material possessions when instead what really matters - at least to me - is the chance to spend time with the people we love while they are alive, or the chance to help improve their lives.

The value I attach to money or worldly possessions is directly proportional to how many lives they improve. Otherwise, they are useless.

You start to see things differently the moment you realize we’re all going to die - sooner than we’d like to believe.
